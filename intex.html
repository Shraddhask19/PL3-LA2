About My City

Satara

Satara is a city located in the Satara District of Maharashtra state of India, near the confluence of the river Krishna and its tributary, the Venna.
What is unique about Satara?
Image result for about satara Satara is famous for the Kaas Plateau, Thoseghar, and a lot of natural sites present in the vicinity of the city. Kass Plateau has been awarded as a UNESCO's World Heritage Site (WHS). In the monsoon months, the Kaas Pathar, as it is locally known, transforms into a wildflower wonderland.



Places to visit in Satara

The Ajinkyatara fort is one of the most important pillars in the history of Maharashtra.


Kaas plateau or Kaas Pathar also called as valley of flowers of Maharashtra, situated 25 km west from Satara city.


Thoseghar Waterfalls is the tallest waterfalls of Maharashtra.
